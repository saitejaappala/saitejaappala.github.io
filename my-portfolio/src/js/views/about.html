Executive Summary

I'm a Senior Oracle Cloud Consultant at Deloitte — but the story started long before the job title. Growing up with a deep curiosity for how technology shapes the way organisations work, I pursued Computer Science at NIT Andhra Pradesh, where four years of study ran parallel to four years of building things from scratch: alumni networks, TEDx events, Google Developer communities, and inter-college conclaves. That experience of making complex things happen with people is something I carry directly into my work today.

Since joining Deloitte in 2020, I've focused on one discipline with real depth — Oracle Cloud PaaS. I build VBCS applications that replace what standard Oracle can't deliver, design OIC integrations that connect fragmented enterprise systems, author BI Publisher and OTBI reports that transform how Finance and Procurement teams make decisions, and write the PL/SQL and Unix automation that keeps everything running reliably behind the scenes. What drives me isn't the technology itself — it's the gap between what Oracle ships out of the box and what businesses actually need. My best work lives in that gap.

Currently pursuing an MBA in Business Analytics at BITS Pilani alongside my work at Deloitte — because I believe the next frontier for Oracle Cloud consultants isn't just technical depth, it's the ability to translate platform capability into strategic business value. Open to senior roles and leadership opportunities where I can keep building at that intersection.
