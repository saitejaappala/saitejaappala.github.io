Program Setup — Custom PaaS Application
Enterprise Client · Deloitte · 2023–2024
When the standard Oracle Program page couldn't support the field depth or audit trail the business required, I architected a custom PaaS replacement from scratch. Program Setup Information — built on Oracle VBCS — handles all three program types (Parent, Child, Standalone) with hierarchy management, an advanced filter-driven search home screen, and full View, Create, and Update workflows. Data flows from Standard Oracle REST APIs, lookups, and value sets into Oracle ATP. With 40+ cross-field validations and complex people fragments, it's one of the most technically demanding apps I've delivered — and the one that finally gave the business the complete audit history the native page never could.
